bibimbap recipe

Ingredient

  • rice
  • Gochugang
  • egg
  • any vegetables and meats you like
  • seasame oil

Steps

  1. Put the rice in a bowl
  2. Slice vegetables and meats and stir fry it
  3. Put stir fied vegerables and meats over rice
  4. Prepare sunny side up egg and put it over rice
  5. put some gocuchang and seasame oil
  6. mix it and eat it
