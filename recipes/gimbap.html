gimbap recipe

Good and simple food for picnic

In Korea, we usually eat gimbap when we don't have time

or when we want to eat

Ingredient

  • gim
  • rice
  • eggs
  • any vegetables you like
  • any meats you like

Steps

  1. Prepare egg gidan and cut it
  2. Slice vegetables and pan fry it and cool it
  3. Slice meats and pan fry it and cool it
  4. put gim amd make it flat
  5. put rice over the gim
  6. put sliced egg gidan, vegetables and meat
  7. roll it and slice it into several pieces
